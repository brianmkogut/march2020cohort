A little about Joye and her Life

Joye says that she feels lke she was so lucky to grow up in "perfect harmony", being raised by both parents who were famous, professional, working musicians in New Orleans. They were making a difference with their music! Joye's mom, Marion Hebert, who played the trumpet, created the first all female band in the early 1930's called "Marion Hebert and her all girl orchestra". Her father played the saxophone and clarinet in Val Barbara's band on the Steamer President. This helped Joye develop a true love for music and she started practicing in her living room, mastering the trumpet, guitar, piano, and drums. Joye later played drums professionally in a group called "Sister Beat" and in a rock band called "The Rainbow". Joye then realized she wanted to make a difference not only with her music but with specific lifestyle changes she experienced herself. As a result of her lifetime of healthy living and education, Joye would love to share with you her innovative and highly successful path to health and well-being.
